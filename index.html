About the Hackathon

First Hackathon – First you

Unique event where all the greatest IT minds of Montenegro will come together to invent, develop and bring to live their brightest ideas to make everyone’s lives better.

24 hours

For 24 hours your team will implement ideas you will come upon. If you have none, we have a few ideas for you! You may also join any teams that may miss your skills, to create an outstanding service that will simplify life for all us.

Aim is to simplify our day-to-day life

Be it an app, chatbot, web platform or something else - we consider all that! The aim of this Hackathon is to simplify and ease our day-to-day life at Montenegro (or maybe in the whole world?), so we can all focus on more important thing rather than how to order a taxi or transfer money to your grandma in Brazil!
